Pricing & Commission

Understand the math behind payments and cost at Seventh Square.

Basics

Sale Price

Amount paid by the customer on purchase of product

Marketplace Commission

Includes Commission based on Product,Payement collection fee (if applicable)

Service GST 18%

GST applicable on Marketplace Commission

Settlement Amount

Amount transferrable to your account for the product sale

For example, if price paid by the customer is INR 1000 (excluding delivery) and Product commission is 5% and payment collection 3%, then the calculation would look something like this below (delivery charges are separately settled in this case)

1000
80 (50 + 30)
14 (80 X 18%)
906

How does delivery work?

Sellers have the option to deliver the products themselves or with us. Here's how it looks :
1. Inclusive Delivery : Delivery charges are included in the product sale price and customer pays no extra shipping.
2. Exclusive Delivery : Delivery charges are excluded from sale price and customer pays shipping extra as per location and product weight
Both these options are available whether seller chooses self shipping or Shipping with Seventh Square

What is payment collection fee ?

This is the fee charged by payment gateways and delivery operators for collection of payment. This is only applicable if the customer pays using payment gateway on the platform or by cash on delivery. This is flat at 3% and is charged from the sale price of the product along with 18% GST on it.
