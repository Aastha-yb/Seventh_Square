Orders FAQs

1.  What happens if I don't have the product in inventory to deliver any order?

Although it is not mandatory for sellers to keep inventory updated, we advise you to keep sufficient inventory in hand to fulfill any orders you get from Seventh Square. If you can’t deliver for any order, it is marked under seller cancellation.

2.  How will I know when a customer orders?

You’ll be notified on your registered email and mobile number. Your seller dashboard will also show complete information about any orders.

3.  I’m not receiving order email notifications. What do I do?

Please reach out at seller@seventhsq.com or write to us here and we’ll get it fixed.

4.  Can I get an order report?

Yes. You can easily get the order report for duration of your choice. Send a simple mail to Seller Help at seller@seventhsq.com with your desired duration and we’ll do the rest within 24 hours.
